Portal Challenge Story

Jump to your fun!

Jerry is a forty-five year-old US citizen living in the city of Seattle. A discredited scientist by the scientific community, believing in the existence of other dimensions, beyond the dimension in which he lives. Jerry has been researching for twenty years, but not succeeded in proving his theory, until now. To develop your project, in which he calls it the "portal weapon". Due to a period of conflict in your country, the government, in an attempt to soften it, creates a law that allows crimes for twelve hours. Now my friend Murilo will explain a bit more about the context in which Jerry is inserted, that is, what he will do during these crime nights and what our player's goal will be. During one of those "crime nights", Jerry found himself at a standstill, go looking for the missing pieces and, try to complete his research, or stay home locked up. Jump with Jerry as far as you can in search of the secret to complete the "Gate Weapon." Take on challenges in the test lab, jump over obstacles, overcome the dangers and, most importantly, teleport yourself for fun!
